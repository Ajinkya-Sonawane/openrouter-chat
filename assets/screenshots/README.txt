Screenshots Directory

Place screenshots of the app in this directory with the following names:
1. chat-screen.png - Screenshot of the ChatScreen with messages
2. model-selection.png - Screenshot of the ModelSelectionScreen
3. settings.png - Screenshot of the SettingsScreen
4. chat-list.png - Screenshot of the ChatListScreen

Recommended specifications:
- Resolution: 1080x1920 or your device's native resolution
- Format: PNG
- Content: Show the app with real content (conversations, models, etc.)
- Crop: Make sure to capture just the app, not device status bars

These screenshots will be used in the README.md to showcase the app's features.
